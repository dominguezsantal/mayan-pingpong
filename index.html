Game Objective:

This game will allow you to: find out the name of the month you were born in Mayan and it will count up to your number from 1. They're a few exceptions in the game including:

  1. Numbers divisible by 3 will pop message "ping"
  2. Numbers divisible by 5 will pop message "pong"
  3. Numbers divisible by 15 will pop message "ping-pong"
